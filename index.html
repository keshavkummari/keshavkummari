About Us

My Name is Keshav Kummari

I live in Hyderabad, India. I'm a Consultant and Trainer in Linux, Python, Cloud(AWS & AZURE) & DevOps Technologies.
I'm a big Advocate of Agile and DevOps techniques in all the projects I work on.
I held various roles from Banking, Telecome to startups.
I have a background in Unix/Linux, Networking, Security, Programming and distributed Computing.
Nowdays I specialize in everything that has to do with Cloud and DevOps.

Education has been very important over my entire career as in many situations it has been an eye opener. I hold many certifications from Linux(RHCSA & RHCE), AWS, & AZURE.
